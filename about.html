About

The "Why"

Hi, I'm Aidan Lenahan, a junior at Red Bank Regional High School, and I've been walking dogs professionally since 2021. I have always been a "dog person," which is why I started to walk dogs and also why I was eager to start a donation drive to help dogs who may not be comfortable or content.

Inspiration

I was inspired to structure this donation drive the way it is by Dermer Dreams, a local food drive founded in Fair Haven, NJ, which collects their donations by distributing bags and flyers onto the doorsteps of neighbors. On a certain date, the neighbors are told to put their bags outside and the neighborhood captains who signed up to collect the bags will do so and drop them off at a centralized location. At the end of the day, these items are sent off to several different organizations, including Lunchbreak and other local food banks.
